The Legend Of Zelda: Crystal Sword

My first big project. Modeled after the original Legend of Zelda for the NES. In addition, the goal was to mimic the mechanics of the original game as much as possible, but make the map, dungeons, enemies, and story different. Start your adventure in front of a cave, with an old man who gives advice and a sword to help you on your way. This game features about 20 hours of first-time game play, with 9 dungeons, and a large overworld map.

Version 1.0 - This game is available for Windows, and Mac. You can download the executablehere

Megaman Z

A fan made platformer made in homage to the Megaman games for the NES. Has many of the same mechanics as Megaman 2 and 3, However, there are 8 distinct and new robot masters/stages, with a larger stage for Dr. Wily's castle.

Version 2.0 - This game is available for Windows, and Mac. You can download the executablehere

Metroid: Federation Invasion

It's not built from scratch, but it is a Rom hack of Super Metroid. Rom hacks use all the sprites, game mechanics, and tiles of the original game, but most of the time have substantial changes added to the map and progression. Such is the case with this hack. Designed to be around the same amount of time to complete as the original game. Features all of the bosses from the original with a few added twists.

Version 1.1 - This game is available for Windows and Mac via SNES emulator. You can download the hackhere
