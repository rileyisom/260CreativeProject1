About Me

Hey there! I'm Riley Isom and I'm a game developer! I mostly work solo, but occasionally get help and advice from family members. I'm a huge Nintendo fan, so I'm currently making fan games of their earlier NES releases. With that being said, I like 2D platformers and that is my current focus. Everything about my work is on this website!

My Favorite Games

My hobby of being a developer came from me being a player. As stated above, I have loved everything Nintendo since I was a child. My favorite franchise is the Legend of Zelda, but I also love the 3D Mario games, with Super Mario Sunshine being my favorite. I've also taken a liking to Super Metroid, and I've been speedrunning it for fun recently.
